## Philosophy

**Core Belief:** Most technology problems are people and process problems in disguise.

Infrastructure is boring because it should be. Security is only interesting when it's broken. Governance isn't about tools—it's about answering a business question: "Who should have access to what, and why?" Once you answer that, the tools are trivial.

**On Compliance:** Compliance isn't optional in regulated industries (law firms, healthcare, finance). But compliance is also cheap—it's just boring. The expensive part is the leadership work deciding what the rule actually *means* for your organization.

**On AI & Governance:** AI doesn't create security problems. It reveals them. Copilot didn't leak your salary spreadsheet—your permissions already shared it. Copilot just made finding it trivial.
